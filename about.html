About



The intention of the RISE | Interactive experience is take the Decathlon experience beyond the actual tour site. How could we share the story, the experience with even more people? Bringing the tour experience to the world, Like many construction projects, the roots of this web application has evolved and finally settled in one key component. How to share the story of the home along with the data output by the home devices? In a technology envionrment where we have greater access to massive volumes of data...How do we share this story? Combining the data sources and stories from 5+ smart devices, a mountain to climb given the different technologies used by providers. Our hope is that this experience captured the story, the REALLY cool data story as well as showcased the volume of love and detail put in by the team in this project.
